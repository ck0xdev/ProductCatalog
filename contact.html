Legal & Policies

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you place an order with us.

Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp or Email to place an order, we collect essential information required to fulfill your request, including your name, shipping address, and phone number.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for processing your payments, arranging delivery, and communicating with you regarding your order status. We respect your privacy and will never sell or share your personal information with unauthorized third parties.

Terms and Conditions

By placing an order with us, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Payments & Order Confirmation

To successfully confirm your order, an advance payment is strictly required. Customers must pay 100% in advance, or at least an 80% advance deposit. Orders will not be processed until this payment is received.

2. Shipping & Delivery

We pride ourselves on fast fulfillment! Once your order is confirmed and the advance payment is verified, you can expect the delivery of your items within 1 to 2 days.

3. Returns & Refunds Policy

Due to the nature of our products, all sales are final. Products are strictly non-returnable.

Exception for Defective Items: If a product arrives broken, damaged, or defective from our side, we will gladly process a return and issue a refund.

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: To be eligible for this defect refund, you must submit an unboxing video proof. The video must clearly show the sealed package being opened for the first time and immediately display the defect on the product. Without this continuous unboxing video proof, no return or refund claims will be accepted under any circumstances.
